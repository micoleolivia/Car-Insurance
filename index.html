What's driving your risk?

Methodology: Risk factors are derived from the RTMC State of Road Safety Report 2025 (January–March 2025, covering 2024–2025 data). Age factors reflect exact fatality percentages adjusted for population exposure by age band. Province factors are fatality percentages adjusted for registered vehicle share. Gender factor reflects the exact 2025 split of 75.7% male / 21.4% female fatalities. Weekend multiplier derived from 2025 day-of-week crash distribution (Sat+Sun = 45.6% of crashes). Premium estimates are indicative only and are not a quote. Actual premiums vary by insurer.

Data source: Road Traffic Management Corporation (RTMC), State of Road Safety in South Africa, January–March 2025.
